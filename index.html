Biography

Bo Wang is currently a PhD Candidate in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Villanova University. He obtained his Master of Science (MSc.) degree from Shenyang Institute of Automation, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, China in 2018. His research interests include nonlinear control theory (robust, adaptive, passive, sliding-mode, etc.), underactuated systems, nonholonomic systems, multi-agent systems and robotics etc. He has been a reviewer for Journal of Vibration and Control and Aircraft Engineering and Aerospace Technology. He is a student member of IEEE-CSS, and ASME.
